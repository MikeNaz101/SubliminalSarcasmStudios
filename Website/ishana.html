A Tale of Ascension

In a world where divinity is something that can be stolen, Ishana is a 'Godling'—a mortal born with the spark of the divine. But power attracts enemies, and the old gods do not share their thrones willingly.

Ishana - The Godling is a narrative-driven RPG that focuses on player choice, moral ambiguity, and the cost of power. Will you protect the mortals who worship you, or sacrifice them to fuel your ascension?

Gameplay Pillars

Divine Powers

Unlock elemental abilities and reality-bending miracles as your follower count grows.

Reactive World

Towns and NPCs react to your alignment. A benevolent Godling is loved; a tyrant is feared.

Deep Lore

Explore ancient ruins and forgotten temples to uncover the history of the usurped gods.

Tactical Combat

Combine weapon mastery with divine magic to overcome mythological beasts and rival Godlings.
