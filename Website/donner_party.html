Description

Embark on a perilous journey west in this thrilling re-imagining of the classic trail adventure. Manage resources, make tough decisions, and face the harsh realities of the frontier. This is a multi-player, vote-driven experience where cooperation and communication determine survival. Will your party reach its destination, or will you succumb to the dangers of the trail?

Development Journey: What's Working

The core game loop is now stable, and significant progress has been made on the survival mechanics:

Working Features:

  • Core Game Loop & Persistence: The game successfully manages session state, player joins, character selection, and progress using MongoDB.
  • Caravan Movement: The host screen accurately tracks the caravan on the map (via Phaser) and animates movement between predetermined **Waypoints** after each event resolution.
  • Player Decision System: Players vote on scenarios via a streamlined, **tabbed mobile UI**. The scenario window maintains a fixed size, ensuring optimal mobile usability.
  • Weighted Outcomes: Event results are determined by a weighted random system influenced by **Character Class Modifiers** and **Item Usage**.
  • Inventory & Degradation: Players manage **Personal Inventory** (with conditional items like Rope/Knife) and **Shared Supplies**. Items degrade upon failed use (condition) or deplete (ammo).
  • Complex Trading: A functional **Player-to-Player Trading** system allows players to offer personal items and money to the shared party funds, complete with server-side timers and acceptance logic.
  • Survival Mechanics (Passive): **Hunger, Thirst, and Sanity** stats for individual players degrade automatically per turn ("Survival Tick"), increasing game pressure and forcing item consumption.
  • Easter Egg Logic: Secret combinations of votes and items (e.g., using a Rope during a Fight encounter) can trigger unique, narrative-driven outcomes.

Current Development Focus

We are currently focused on Player UI, balancing weighted outcomes, and expanding the survival pressure to make the journey a true challenge:

Immediate Goals:

  • Resource Depletion & Penalty Balancing: Fine-tuning the rate of Hunger, Thirst, and Sanity degradation and ensuring resulting health penalties are fair yet challenging.
  • Sickness Management: Integrating the sick members percentage into the event outcomes and creating specific medical events.
  • Caravan Structural Integrity: Implementing events that damage wagons and require repair items (using the Blacksmith's skills) to mitigate disaster.
  • Historical Event Expansion: Developing a robust set of historically accurate, branching scenarios related to the Oregon Trail and the Donner Party's key struggles.
